# Paper Hearts
## Cocoa Shawnee
### 2015-09-08

Paper hearts,
can't break.
Even after all,
the damage they take.
A tear can be,
sealed back together.
A rip can be,
fastened with tape.
If shredded,
they can be repaired by paste.
But a real heart,
can be broken.
So think before,
any words...are spoken.
